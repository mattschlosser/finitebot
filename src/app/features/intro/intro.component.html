Chalk Boom!

Chalk boom is a game of strategy and luck.

Demo



Adding to your stream.

Simply add your username to the URL, and add it as a browser source.

For example, e.g. https://chalk-boom.netlify.app/ruggmatt

Gameplay

Game play is simple. To join the game, type '!me' in chat. You will be assigned a random color (blue or red). The goal of the game is to cover the chalk board with as much as your team's color as possible. Your chalk meter will be displayed underneath your name. Use '!boom' to boom your chalk and cover the board with your color. The more full your meter is, the more chalk you'll drop. The team with the greatest area of the chalkboard covered in their color wins.

Commands

  • !me - join the chalk boom game
  • !boom - drop some chalk on the board
